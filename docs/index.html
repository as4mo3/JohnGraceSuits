The Three Steps to a John Grace Suit

Planning

The first part of the process starts with planning, and choosing your type of suit. Our tailors help you decide which fabric, cut, and style would fit you the best, and we make a working model for your suit.

Learn More

Measurement and 3-D Printing

Our tailors then take your measurements with a 3-D camera, a process which takes less than 20 minutes. We then print the 3-D model in order to be able to fit your suit perfectly. This whole process takes less than two days, much faster than it would in shop.
